Our Love Story

Two hearts, two cultures, one beautiful love story

💕

How We Met

Our paths crossed in the most unexpected way during a summer festival in Austin. James was visiting from California, and Lily was showing her friend around the city. What started as a chance encounter at a food truck turned into hours of conversation about travel, dreams, and our shared love for adventure.

🌎

Long Distance Love

From California to Mexico, our love grew stronger across borders. Video calls became our lifeline, and every visit felt like a celebration. We learned that love knows no boundaries and that distance only made our hearts grow fonder. Through different time zones and languages, we built something beautiful.

💍

The Proposal

James planned the perfect proposal during Lily's visit to San Francisco. On a foggy morning at Crissy Field with the Golden Gate Bridge in the background, he got down on one knee and asked the question that would change our lives forever. Through happy tears and "Sí, mi amor!", we knew this was just the beginning of our greatest adventure.

🎉

Why Mexico City?

Mexico City holds a special place in our hearts. It's where Lily grew up, where her family creates the most incredible memories, and where James fell in love with Mexican culture. From the vibrant markets to the warm hospitality, this city represents the perfect blend of our two worlds coming together.

About Our Wedding

Celebrating love where two cultures become one

🏛️

The Venue

We're getting married at a beautiful historic hacienda in Mexico City, surrounded by lush gardens and colonial architecture. The venue perfectly captures the romance of old Mexico with modern elegance, offering breathtaking views and an intimate atmosphere for our special day.

🎭

Cultural Celebration

Our wedding will be a beautiful fusion of American and Mexican traditions. From mariachi music to exchanging vows in both English and Spanish, we're creating a celebration that honors both of our heritages and brings our families together in the spirit of love and unity.

🍽️

Food & Festivities

Get ready for an incredible culinary journey! We'll be serving authentic Mexican cuisine alongside California favorites. Think handmade tortillas, mole, fresh ceviche, and signature cocktails featuring both tequila and craft beer. The celebration continues with dancing under the stars!

💒

The Ceremony

Our ceremony will take place in a beautiful garden courtyard at sunset, with heartfelt vows shared in both languages. We'll incorporate meaningful traditions like the lazo ceremony and arras, creating a spiritual and emotional celebration of our commitment to each other and our families.
